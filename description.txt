DESTINY MOD
-----

Destiny 2 is a game that I have grown to love over my 7 years of playing. And while recent events have left me jaded for the worst, I was compelled to recreate the game in my image through modding.
And now, after several years, here we are.

DISCLAIMERS
-----

- Please do not attempt to use this with unverified mods, such as most content or QoL mods.
- Doing the above will likely lead to immediate crashes or errors.

- This is NOT an official port of the Destiny Mod developed by Cuno, Ghost and Smug. This is, and shall be treated as, a separate project.
